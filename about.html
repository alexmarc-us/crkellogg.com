About

C.R. Kellogg is a mom and writer who lives in the Pacific Northwest. Kellogg writes speculative fiction, essays and creative non-fiction. Her fiction explores human connection within speculative environments. She is an alumnus of the Viable Paradise workshop and a member of the Science Fiction Writers of America. Her work has been published in numerous literary magazines and anthologies, most recently Frontiers of Fright: A Southwestern Horror Anthology. Her manuscript won third place in the 2024 Pacific Northwest Writers Association unpublished novel contest in Speculative Fiction.
